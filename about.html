about semak

SEMAC Medical Services Company is a leading company in the field of providing medical, educational, scientific and technical operation and maintenance services. Since its establishment in 2006, SEMAC has gained distinguished experience through the implementation of a group of giant projects in public and private hospitals and universities. We have taken it upon ourselves to provide innovative and distinguished health, scientific and technical services to our customers to contribute to providing a healthy and scientific life for the community and to improve the level of health, scientific and educational services provided in the private and government sectors.
